From Hurricane Helene to Durham Home

I never expected Hurricane Helene to change my life so dramatically. When the storm displaced me from Brevard in Western North Carolina, where I was working as a contractor, friends in Durham offered me a place to stay.

What started as temporary shelter became something beautiful - I fell in love with Durham and its incredible community. When I posted on a local Facebook group looking for work, the response completely blew me away. The warmth and support from Durham neighbors was unlike anything I'd experienced.

That community response led me to officially launch Stillpoint Builders, and I've been busy ever since. Working with Durham homeowners has been an absolute joy - there's something special about this community that makes every project meaningful.

My vision for Stillpoint Builders goes beyond just building decks.

I'm creating outdoor sanctuaries where families gather, friends connect, communities come together, and individuals find solitude. Spaces for what truly matters.

Questions Durham Homeowners Ask

What makes your deck construction different from other Durham contractors?

We use gold standard construction techniques that exceed building codes - superior fastening systems, advanced joist spacing, and moisture protection designed for Triangle weather. The difference is visible in every detail and ensures your deck performs beautifully for decades, not just years.

How do I know you'll actually show up and finish on time?

We document our timeline reliability with every customer. When we give you a start date and completion timeline, that's exactly what happens. Our reputation in Durham depends on doing what we promise, and we're building relationships for the long term.

What about pricing - any surprises or hidden costs?

Our quotes are detailed and transparent. We explain exactly what's included and stick to our pricing. No surprise charges, no hidden costs, no change orders unless you request additional work. You'll know the total investment upfront.

Can you help me avoid Durham's permit process?

Yes! Our fast-track no-permit solutions (decks under 30" height and 200 sq ft) let you skip Durham's permit process entirely. We can start as soon as next week. For larger projects requiring permits, we handle all the paperwork and inspections for you.

How do your techniques handle Triangle weather?

Our gold standard construction includes advanced moisture protection, proper drainage systems, and fastening methods designed specifically for our climate. We use techniques that account for humidity, temperature swings, and storm conditions common in the Triangle area.

What if there are problems after completion?

We stand behind our work and remain available for questions or seasonal care guidance. Our goal is creating outdoor spaces that continue exceeding expectations year after year. We're building long-term relationships in Durham, not just decks.
